Privacy Policy for ChaosVideoPlayer

Last Updated: Today's date

1. Information We Collect

ChaosVideoPlayer accesses:

  • YouTube Live Chat messages (only when you type !play commands)
  • Your YouTube channel information (to verify live stream status)

2. How We Use Information

  • To read !play commands from YouTube Live chat
  • To search and play YouTube videos based on chat requests
  • To display your YouTube channel name (for attribution)

3. What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information beyond your public YouTube username
  • No chat messages other than !play commands
  • No payment or financial information
  • No email addresses (except for support contact)

4. Data Storage

We do not store any user data. All data processing happens in real-time and is not saved.
